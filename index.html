About

A career built on communication, driven by curiosity.

Julie is a research and strategic communications professional with a multidisciplinary background in journalism, healthcare interpretation, research support, community engagement, and digital communication.

She is currently expanding her technical skill set through self-directed learning in Python, HTML/CSS, GitHub, and AI-assisted workflows, with a long-term interest in human-centered technology for healthcare and public communication.
